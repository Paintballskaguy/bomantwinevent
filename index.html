About Us

Located just off the Broken Arrow Expressway at 31st and Sheridan, Boman Twin Event Center provides a versatile 10,500 sq ft venue featuring a full plug-and-play PA system with lighting and a stage, making it ideal for concerts, raves, conferences, gaming conventions, and private parties. Our spacious main room offers 5,500 sq ft of flexible open space, accommodating up to 600 guests with customizable arrangements.

We cater to a wide range of events and audiences, from concerts and quinceañeras to trade shows and birthday parties. With easy access to major highways, ample parking, and nearby hotels and dining options, our location ensures convenience for every occasion. Our full-service bar, catering prep area, and adaptable amenities are ready to support your event's unique requirements.
